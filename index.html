About our tissue culture plant

We specialize in high‑quality banana tissue culture plants. Every plantlet is produced in a clean lab, hardened in our nursery, and supplied ready for field planting.

Why tissue culture?

Uniform, vigorous growth

Tissue culture plants are genetically uniform, disease‑free, and give better yield compared to conventional suckers.

Our process

From lab to your field

Plantlets are multiplied in vitro, then hardened in controlled shade‑net conditions before dispatch, ensuring strong survival in your farm.
